# Content Positioning

This skill packages AI news as knowledge-card content, not as raw news reposting.

## Position

- Explain AI hotspots in plain language.
- Turn facts into one clear reader takeaway.
- Help readers understand "what changed" and "why it matters".
- Keep the tone like a calm AI product note or science explainer.

## Not News Reposting

- Do not translate or rewrite the whole article.
- Do not copy paragraphs from a source.
- Do not make the carousel a timeline of every detail.
- Do not bury the reader in company statements or technical specs.

## Not Clickbait

- Avoid exaggerated claims such as "颠覆一切", "全行业变天", "普通人必须马上学会".
- Avoid fear-driven wording.
- Avoid pretending a product update is bigger than the evidence supports.

## Desired Reader Experience

After reading, a non-expert should be able to say:

- I know what happened.
- I know what this means for normal users or creators.
- I know why product people or designers may care.
- I know what still needs verification.

# Writing Style Rules

## Voice

- Write in Chinese by default.
- Sound like a thoughtful AI practitioner explaining the point to a smart friend.
- Be clear, calm, and slightly opinionated.
- Use "你以为 / 其实" only where it improves understanding.

## Sentence Shape

- Prefer short sentences.
- Keep each page body under 80 Chinese characters when possible.
- Use one idea per page.
- Break complicated ideas into 2-3 short points.

## Technical Terms

- Use technical terms only when needed.
- Explain the first appearance in plain Chinese.
- Prefer "它能同时理解文字、图片和声音" over "端到端多模态能力".

## Opinion Boundaries

- Say "可能意味着", "更像是", "可以理解为" when interpreting.
- Do not state speculation as fact.
- Do not turn one product update into an industry-wide conclusion without support.

## Avoid

- Press-release tone.
- Long article paragraphs.
- Dense jargon.
- Emotional shouting.
- Excessive emoji.
- Overly broad claims.
- Fear or urgency hooks.

## Good Patterns

- "重点不是功能变多，而是入口变近。"
- "它不像一个按钮，更像一个会预判需求的助理。"
- "普通人能感受到的变化，是少点几步。"
- "对产品经理来说，这更像一次流程重排。"

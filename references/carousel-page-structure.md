# Carousel Page Structure

Default to 6 pages unless the user requests a different length.

## Default 6-Page Structure

1. Cover
   - Stop the reader with one clear idea.
   - Include title, subtitle, column label, and small tags.

2. Myth
   - Present the common misunderstanding.
   - Use "你以为 / 其实" contrast.

3. Metaphor
   - Explain the topic through a daily-life analogy.
   - Avoid technical overload.

4. Breakdown
   - Split the update into 2-3 short points.
   - Make the copy suitable for bullet layout.

5. Impact
   - Explain why it matters.
   - Pick the best audience angle.

6. Recap
   - Summarize with 3-4 small recap cards.
   - End with a calm final sentence or comment prompt.

## Alternative: AI News Quick Read

Use when the user wants a fast news card:

1. What happened.
2. One sentence explanation.
3. Why it matters.
4. Who is affected.
5. What to watch next.
6. Recap.

## Alternative: AI Tool Update

Use when the topic is a product feature:

1. What changed.
2. Old workflow vs new workflow.
3. Where the feature appears.
4. Who benefits.
5. What it signals.
6. Try-it checklist.

## Alternative: AI Concept Explainer

Use when explaining a concept rather than news:

1. Concept name.
2. Common misunderstanding.
3. Plain-language metaphor.
4. Core mechanism.
5. Everyday example.
6. One-line recap.

## Alternative: Product Manager Perspective

Use when the user asks for a product analysis angle:

1. Product signal.
2. User pain point.
3. Workflow change.
4. Interaction or entry-point change.
5. Business or platform meaning.
6. PM takeaway.
